Gallery V is a conceptual vintage store, specialising in hand-picked vintage mens and women's garments varying from t-shirts, sweats, jackets and small miscellaneous items such as jewellery. In addition to the e-commerce side, the concept also includes a blog with articles based on the vintage fashion culture. My aim for this project was to use my affinity with music and film with a sole purpose of selling rare promotional material, ranging from hard to find band t-shirts to archived fashion pieces.
